VI. Special Motion (The "Beyond Straight Line" Branch)

Uniform Circular Motion (UCM)

Definition: When an object moves in a circular path with uniform speed.

Nature: This motion is always Accelerated Motion because the direction of velocity changes continuously (at every point), even though the speed is constant.

Formula for Speed (v):

v = 2πr
t

(where r is the radius and t is the time for one revolution).
