I. Summary Table

Graph Type Axes (Y-Axis vs. X-Axis) Motion Type Look of the Graph What the Slope Represents
Distance-Time Distance (m) vs. Time (s) Rest Horizontal line (parallel to x-axis) Speed (is zero)
Distance-Time Distance (m) vs. Time (s) Uniform Speed Straight line with a positive slope Speed (is constant)
Velocity-Time Velocity (m/s) vs. Time (s) Uniform Velocity (Zero Acceleration) Horizontal line (parallel to x-axis) Acceleration (is zero)
Velocity-Time Velocity (m/s) vs. Time (s) Uniform Acceleration Straight line with a positive slope (from origin/y-intercept) Acceleration (is constant)
Area under V-T Graph — — Area under the velocity line Represents **Displacement**
