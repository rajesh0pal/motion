Mind Map Component: Definitions & Concepts (The "What is it?" Branch)

This section lists the fundamental concepts from the chapter on Motion, their precise definitions, standard symbols, and SI units.

Concept Definition (What it is) Symbol SI Unit
Distance Total path length covered. s or d metre (m)
Displacement Shortest distance from initial to final position. s or d metre (m)
Speed Distance travelled per unit time. v m/s or m s-1
Velocity Speed in a definite direction (Rate of change of displacement). v m/s or m s-1
Acceleration Rate of change of velocity. a m/s2 or m s-2
Average Speed Total distance divided by total time. vavg m/s or m s-1
